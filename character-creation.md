# Character Creation

Create a character by rolling a D6 three times. The rolls determine attributes of Age, Religious Background and Wealth.

## Age
|Roll|Attribute|
|---|---|
|1|10-15|
|2|16-20|
|3|21-25|
|4|26-30|
|5|31-40|
|6|41-50|

## Religious Background

|Roll|Attribute|
|---|---|
|1|Catholic|
|2|Catholic|
|3|Protestant|
|4|Non Religious|
|5|Other Abramhamic|
|6|Player Chooses|
